Getting Around Granada

Walking + Taxis + Bus C30
Alhambra Bus
Bus C30 (red minibus) — departs Plaza Nueva every 8–10 minutes. €1.60 single or €0.54 with Credibus card. Journey ~10 min to the Alhambra entrance. Much easier than the steep walk.

For the 8:30 AM Alhambra entry: take a taxi (~€7–10) to guarantee timing.
Bus Network
Operated by ALSA/Rober. 25 daytime + 2 night lines. Single ticket: €1.60 (pay driver). Credibus reloadable card: €0.54/journey (top up with €5/10/20, €2 card deposit refundable). Shareable between two people.
Key lines
C30 & C32: both go to Alhambra from Plaza Nueva. C31: Albaicín. Buy Credibus card on any red bus or at tobacco kiosks (estancos).
Metro
Granada has a tram/metro but it does NOT reach the historic centre or Alhambra — not useful for tourists. The C30 bus is your Alhambra transport.
Taxis
White with green diagonal stripe and coat of arms. Very cheap for short distances (~€5–8 across centre). Pide Taxi Granada: +34 958 28 00 00 (24hrs). Radio Taxi Genil: +34 958 132 323 (24hrs).
Hotel → Alhambra
Taxi: ~€7–10, 10 min. Bus C30 from Plaza Nueva (5-min walk from hotel): €1.60, 10 min. For 8:30 AM entry — take a taxi.
App
Google Maps works well for bus routes. Download the ALSA app for real-time departures.
